Security Plans

Basic Security

  • Establish Physical Security Mission
  • Development of a comprehensive physical security plan outline that directly aligns with the organizational mission.
  • Develop a physical security profile that wholly supports COMPANY mission. We will maintain industry best practices that provide awareness to personnel at all locations through the development of standards, policies, and processes that improve the physical security health of our campuses.

Intermediate Security

  • Full Stake Holder engagement
  • Involve stake holders from key areas within the organization (legal, facilities, risk , compliance, etc.) and provide them with a detailed overview and direction for the physical security posture that we intend to establish and maintain.
  • Guide these stake holders through the high level physical security plan in order to solicit a clear understanding of, and ultimately agreed upon, appropriate campus physical security strategies and implementations.

Advanced Security

  • Physical Security assessment
  • Develop a comprehensive assessment tool that encompasses best practices and accommodates the unique qualities of the facilities and communities we serve.

Comprehensive Security

  • the Physical Security Standards
  • Standards all meet or exceed regulatory requirements (e.g. The Joint Commission, The Cleary Act and NERC & FERC.
  • Matrix/Mitigation Strategy
  • Provide a high level overview of our current physical security threat, vulnerability, and risk posture.
  • Develop Repeatable and Sustainable Processes
  • Enable consistency at all of our locations.
  • Require Regular Review
  • Physical Security process that is regularly reviewed to ensure that our level of service is appropriately maintained.
